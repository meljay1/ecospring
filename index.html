    Our Services

    Eco Spring Limited offers the following services.

    Construction Work

    Eco Spring Limited offers a cutting-edge Eco Construction service that integrates sustainable building practices with innovative, environmentally friendly materials. This service is tailored to meet the growing demand for eco-conscious construction solutions, focusing on reducing environmental impact while enhancing building efficiency and durability.

    Paver & Roofing Tiles Installation

    We provide specialized service for the installation of roofing tiles made from recycled plastic. This service is designed to offer a sustainable, durable, and eco-friendly roofing solution for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.

    Waste Management

    We offer a comprehensive Waste Management service focused on the efficient collection, segregation, and recycling of plastic and glass waste. This service is designed to support environmental sustainability by transforming waste into valuable resources while reducing the environmental impact of waste disposal.

    Facility Management

    We offer a comprehensive Facility Management service designed to ensure the efficient and sustainable operation of residential, commercial, and industrial properties. The service integrates a wide range of maintenance, operational, and environmental management solutions to optimize building performance and enhance occupant satisfaction.

    Landscaping

    We offer premium Landscaping services that prioritize sustainability through the use of eco-friendly clay paver and/or recycled plastic pavers. These services are designed to create beautiful, durable, and environmentally conscious outdoor spaces for residential, commercial, and public areas.

    Recycling of Glass

    We collects various types of glass, including bottles, jars, and containers. The glass is sorted, cleaned, and crushed into cullets, which is then used in the production roofing sheets.

    Frequently Asked Questioins

    Below are some of the questions our customer frequently ask.

    • What are Eco Spring's recycled plastic roofing tiles made from?

      Our recycled plastic roofing tiles are made from high-quality, post-consumer plastic waste, crushed glass and sand. The plastic and glass are cleaned, crushed, and a mixture of crushed plastic glass and sand is molded into roofing tiles that are designed to withstand various weather conditions.

    • How durable are these roofing tiles compared to traditional materials?

      Eco Spring's recycled plastic roofing tiles are extremely durable and designed to last for decades. They are resistant to cracking, fading, and weather damage, making them a reliable alternative to traditional roofing materials.

    • Are recycled plastic roofing tiles eco-friendly?

      Are recycled plastic roofing tiles eco-friendly?

    • Can these tiles be used in all climates?

      Yes, our recycled plastic roofing tiles are designed to perform well in a variety of climates, from extreme heat to heavy snowfall. They are UV-resistant and have excellent thermal insulation properties.

    • How do recycled plastic roofing tiles impact energy efficiency?

      These tiles provide excellent insulation, helping to keep buildings cooler in the dry season and warmer in the cold season which can reduce energy costs for heating and cooling.

    • What maintenance is required for these roofing tiles?

      Recycled plastic roofing tiles are low-maintenance. They do not require regular treatments or coatings, and their durability means they are resistant to moss, mold, and mildew. Occasional cleaning to remove debris is typically all that‚Äôs needed.

    • What are the benefits of using clay bricks from Eco Spring?

      Eco Spring‚Äôs clay interlocking bricks are highly durable, thermally efficient, and made from clay and a small amount of cement. They provide excellent insulation, soundproofing, and are resistant to fire, pests, and weathering, making them a long-lasting choice for construction.

    • Are clay bricks environmentally friendly?

      Yes, our clay interlocking bricks are an eco-friendly building material. They are made from natural clay and a minimal amount of cement. The manufacturing process is energy-efficient, and the bricks themselves can be recycled at the end of their life.

    • Are these tiles available in different colors?

      Yes, Eco Spring offers a range of colors to match various architectural designs. You can choose the look that best compliments your building.

    • How do clay bricks contribute to energy efficiency?

      Our clay interlocking bricks have excellent thermal mass, meaning they can absorb and store heat, helping to regulate indoor temperatures. This reduces the need for artificial heating and cooling, leading to lower energy bills.
